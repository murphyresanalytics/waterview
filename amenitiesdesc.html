Basketball & Pickleball Court

Experience our modern outdoor court expertly designed for both basketball and fast-paced pickleball. Perfect for competitive play or casual fun, with evening lighting and equipment available on request.

!Fitness Center!

Fitness Center

Our state-of-the-art fitness center features a wide range of cardio and strength training equipment, open 8AM-10PM for your convenience. Enjoy a clean, modern space designed to help you achieve your fitness goals. The center is equipped with free weights, machines, and a stretching area.

!Fishing Pad!

Fishing Pad

Enjoy a peaceful day of fishing at our scenic fishing pad, complete with seating and shaded areas. Perfect for relaxation or family outings, this tranquil spot is stocked with fish and offers a serene escape. Our fishing pad is designed for all ages, providing a safe and enjoyable environment to connect with nature, and is open 24/7 year-round.

!Pool & Grill!

Pool & Grill Area

Our sparkling outdoor pool is perfect for relaxation and exercise, surrounded by comfortable lounge chairs and umbrellas. Adjacent to the pool, our grill area features modern BBQ grills and picnic tables, ideal for social gatherings and family meals. The pool is open from 8AM to 10PM daily, while the grill area is available from 10AM to 9PM. Both areas are maintained to the highest standards for your enjoyment.

!Pool Rules!

Pool Rules

  • Pool hours are from 8AM to 10PM daily.
  • Children under 14 must be supervised by an adult.
  • No glass containers, food, or drink in the pool area.
  • Shower before entering the pool.
  • No diving or running around the pool.
  • Proper swim attire is required.
  • Please respect other guests and keep noise to a minimum.
  • Use pool at your own risk. We are not responsible for accidents or injuries.
  • No pets allowed in the pool area.
  • No running, pushing or other dangerous horseplay.
  • Management reserves the right to deny use of pool to anyone at any time.
  • Use pool at own risk- no lifeguard on duty.
!Grill Rules!

Grill Rules

  • Please clean grills and grilling area after each use.
  • Check the surrounding area for flammable debris.
  • Supervise the children around the hot grill.
  • Please make sure all knobs are in the off position before leaving grilling area.
  • Please be courteous with your time if others are waiting to use the grills.
!locker room facilities!

Locker Room Facilities

Our modern locker room facilities provide a convenient and secure space to store your belongings while you enjoy our amenities. Equipped with spacious lockers, showers, and changing areas, our locker rooms are designed for comfort and privacy. Access is included with your membership or day pass, ensuring a hassle-free experience every time you visit. Additionally, complimentary toiletries and fresh towels are provided for your convenience. Waterview's locker rooms also contain saunas for relaxation after a workout or swim.

!Pond!

Pond

Our picturesque pond offers a serene escape within the community, perfect for relaxation and enjoying nature. Surrounded by lush landscaping and walking paths, the pond area is ideal for leisurely strolls, picnics, or simply unwinding by the water. The pond is also stocked with fish, making it a popular spot for fishing enthusiasts. Benches and shaded areas are available for your comfort, allowing you to fully appreciate the tranquil ambiance. No swimming allowed in the pond.
