How do I report maintenance issues?
Use the resident portal or call the on-site maintenance team directly.
What is a Land Lease and where do I fill it out to sign onto a unit?
A Land Lease is a legal agreement between the tenant and the property owner. It outlines the terms of occupancy, including rent, duration, and responsibilities. To sign onto a unit, you can fill out the Land Lease form available in the resident portal or at the leasing office.
What is the pet policy?
Pets are allowed, but residents should always clean up after their pets and ensure they are leashed in common areas.
Do the units come furnished?
Each unit is mostly furnished, excluding personal items.
Are there any renovation restrictions?
Major renovations require prior approval from management. Minor changes like painting or decorating are generally allowed.
What do residents get to pick when they sign onto their unit?
Residents have the option to change door color, tile and wall color, choose their own decor, etc.
